Frequently asked questions

Is RentEasy free for tenants?
Yes! Tenants can sign up and post for free, but a 7.5% commission is paid by incoming tenants after a successful rental.
Do I still need an agent?
No. RentEasy connects you directly with landlords or verified local managers. No traditional agent required.
How are listings verified?
A RentEasy manager visits the property, takes photos, and confirms details before the “Verified” badge is issued.
How can I earn money on RentEasy?
Outgoing tenants earn 1.5% when their vacating property gets rented. Managers earn 2.5% per successful rental.
What about estate firms?
Estate firms pay 0% commission for thier listings when subscribed. They also appear in our marketplace for landlords and potential clients to discover.
Is my payment safe?
Payments are made offline between tenant and landlord, monitored and facilitated by the manager. RentEasy charges commission after confirmation.
