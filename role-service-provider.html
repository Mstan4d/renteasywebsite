Why Join as a Service Provider?

  • Free visibility – Appear in the RentEasy marketplace immediately after registration
  • First 10 bookings free – Zero commission on your first 10 service jobs
  • Low-cost subscription – After 10 free bookings, pay a very cheap monthly fee to continue getting leads

How It Works

  1. Download the RentEasy app
  2. Sign up as a "Service Provider"
  3. Complete your profile – add services, photos, pricing, location
  4. Start receiving booking requests from tenants, landlords, and estate firms
  5. First 10 bookings: 0% commission – you keep everything
  6. After 10 bookings: subscribe monthly (very cheap) to continue getting leads
  7. Optional: Buy a "Boost" to appear at the top of search results

Pricing & Commission

  • First 10 bookings: ₦0 commission – 100% of payment goes to you
  • After 10 bookings: Monthly subscription (e.g., ₦2,000 – ₦5,000/month depending on plan)
  • Optional boost: One-time fee for priority ranking
  • No per-booking commission – Just the flat subscription fee
  • You set your own prices – RentEasy doesn't take a cut of your service fees

Service Provider Dashboard

  • Manage your service listings (add/edit/remove services)
  • View incoming booking requests
  • Track your free bookings remaining (countdown from 10)
  • Upgrade to subscription when ready
  • Access customer reviews and ratings
  • View earnings and payment history

Verification & Trust

  • Submit KYC documents (ID, business registration if any)
  • Get a "Verified Service Provider" badge after approval
  • Badge increases customer trust and booking rates
  • Customers can rate and review your work – build your reputation
