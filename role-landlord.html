How It Works for Landlords

  1. Download the RentEasy app and sign up as a Landlord
  2. Post your property for free – add photos, description, rent amount
  3. Your property gets assigned to a local manager (if needed) or tenants contact you directly
  4. When a tenant rents, you pay only the 7.5% commission – no agent fees
  5. You receive the rent payment first, then the commission is deducted

Why Landlords Love RentEasy

  • 0% agent dependency – Tenants contact you directly via in-app chat
  • Verified tenants – Only serious, KYC-verified users can message you
  • Transparent commission – 7.5% total, paid only when rented
  • No hidden charges – What you see is what you get
  • Property stays visible – Until you mark it as rented

Commission Breakdown

  • 2.5% → Local manager (verification & oversight)
  • 1.5% → Referrer (outgoing tenant or whoever posted)
  • 3.5% → RentEasy (platform)
  • You keep 92.5% of the annual rent (after commission)
