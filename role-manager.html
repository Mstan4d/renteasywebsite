Become a Verified RentEasy Manager

Earn 2.5% commission on every property you manage. Flexible, local work with no upfront cost.

What Does a Manager Do?

  • Receive notifications for properties posted in your area (within 1km radius)
  • First to accept becomes the listing manager
  • Visit the property, take photos, verify details (on-site verification)
  • Mark the property as "Verified" – gives tenants confidence
  • Monitor the conversation between tenant and landlord (for landlord-posted listings)
  • Confirm when rent payment is made (ensure the landlord receives payment)
  • Earn 2.5% of the annual rent when the deal closes

Requirements

  • Must pass KYC verification (ID, address proof)
  • Live in the city you want to manage (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt initially)
  • Have a smartphone with camera and internet
  • Good communication skills

Earnings Potential

  • 2.5% of annual rent per successful rental
  • Example: Property rented at ₦1,000,000/year → you earn ₦25,000
  • Manage multiple properties simultaneously – unlimited earnings
  • Paid directly to your bank account after admin verification

Step-by-Step to Become a Manager

  1. Download the RentEasy app
  2. Sign up and select "Become a Manager"
  3. Submit your KYC documents (National ID, proof of address)
  4. Wait for admin approval (usually within 48 hours)
  5. Once verified, you'll start receiving property notifications in your area
  6. Accept a listing, visit the property, verify it
  7. Monitor the transaction, confirm payment, get paid!
