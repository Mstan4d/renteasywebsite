Moving Out? Earn 1.5% Commission When Your Apartment Gets Rented

Post your vacating property on RentEasy and earn a referral bonus – no agent needed.

How It Works

  1. You're moving out of your apartment
  2. Post the property on RentEasy (free, takes 2 minutes)
  3. A local manager verifies the listing and manages the rental process
  4. When a new tenant rents the property, you receive 1.5% of the annual rent
  5. Example: If rent is ₦1,000,000/year → you earn ₦15,000

What to Post

  • Property photos (up to 10)
  • Address, city, landmark
  • Rent amount (annual)
  • Bedrooms, bathrooms, area
  • Your contact info (or landlord's)

Why Post on RentEasy?

  • You earn money for doing almost nothing
  • The property gets verified, so tenants trust it
  • Manager handles the communication and payment collection
  • You don't need to be a landlord – just a tenant moving out
